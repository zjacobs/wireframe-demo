Our History

WUSIF was founded in 2007-2008 with $100,000 from Washington University and the Olin Business School's Skandalaris Center. The club focuses strictly on value investments with an initial horizon of 3-5 years before re-evaluation.




Weekly Meetings

We meet once a week, usually Wednesdays (varies every semester), at 7PM in Simon Hall (Olin Business School). This semester (Fall 2014), we meet Wednesdays in Simon 110.




New Member Education

The club is committed to educating new members on fundamental analysis (including qualitative and quantitative analysis). This allows new members to participate on pitches during their first semester. Meetings for the education sessions are on Sundays at 2PM, and this semester, meetings are in Simon 106. The program culminates with an Analyst Pitch Competition to showcase the knowledge and skills learned throughout the semester.
